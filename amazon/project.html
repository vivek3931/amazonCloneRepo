DISPLAY

Display Size: The iPhone 15 Pro Max features a 6.7-inch OLED Super Retina XDR display. Resolution: The display resolution is 2796 x 1290 pixels at 460 pixels per inch (ppi). Design: The display has rounded corners that follow a beautiful curved design. When measured as a standard rectangular shape, the screen is 6.69 inches diagonally (actual viewable area is slightly less). Key Features: Super Retina XDR: High-quality OLED technology for vibrant colors and deep blacks. ProMotion Technology: Adaptive refresh rates up to 120Hz for smoother animations. Extreme Dynamic Range: Excellent contrast and brightness. True Tone: Adjusts display color temperature based on ambient light. Wide Color (P3): Rich and accurate color reproduction. Haptic Touch: Provides tactile feedback when interacting with the screen. Brightness: Max Brightness (Typical): 1000 nits (for everyday use). Peak Brightness (HDR): 1600 nits (for high dynamic range content). Peak Brightness (Outdoor): 2000 nits (for visibility in bright sunlight).

CAMERA

Main Camera: 48 megapixels with a Sony IMX-803 image sensor. ƒ/1.78 aperture. Second-generation sensor-shift optical image stabilization. Supports super-high-resolution photos (24MP and 48MP). Ultra Wide Camera: 12.7 megapixels. ƒ/2.2 aperture. 120° field of view. Also equipped with 100% Focus Pixels. Telephoto Camera: Enabled by a quad-pixel sensor. 48 mm equivalent focal length. ƒ/1.78 aperture. Second-generation sensor-shift optical image stabilization. Supports 100% Focus Pixels. Additional Features: 5x optical zoom. 120mm focal length. 25% larger telephoto sensor. Utilizes a petra-prism design to enhance light separation between lens and sensor.

PERFORMANCE

Chipset (Processor): The iPhone 15 Pro Max is powered by the A17 Pro chip, a cutting-edge 3nm processor. It features a new 6-core CPU with 2 performance cores and 4 efficiency cores. The GPU is a new 6-core design for exceptional gaming performance, including support for ray tracing. The 16-core Neural Engine enhances machine learning tasks. Console-Quality Gaming: Thanks to the A17 Pro chip, the iPhone 15 Pro Max delivers console-quality gameplay. Gamers can enjoy smooth graphics, high frame rates, and immersive experiences. Battery Life: The battery life on the iPhone 15 Pro Max is impressive, lasting up to an epic 14 hours. Whether you’re gaming, streaming, or working, the phone keeps up with your demands. Overall Performance: With the A17 Pro chip, this flagship phone ensures snappy responsiveness, seamless multitasking, and efficient power management. Whether you’re editing photos, running AR apps, or browsing the web, the iPhone 15 Pro Max delivers top-tier performance.
