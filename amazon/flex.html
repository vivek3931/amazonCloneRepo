Display Size: The iPhone 15 Pro Max features a 6.7-inch OLED Super Retina XDR display. Resolution: The display resolution is 2796 x 1290 pixels at 460 pixels per inch (ppi). Design: The display has rounded corners that follow a beautiful curved design. When measured as a standard rectangular shape, the screen is 6.69 inches diagonally (actual viewable area is slightly less). Key Features: Super Retina XDR: High-quality OLED technology for vibrant colors and deep blacks. ProMotion Technology: Adaptive refresh rates up to 120Hz for smoother animations. Extreme Dynamic Range: Excellent contrast and brightness. True Tone: Adjusts display color temperature based on ambient light. Wide Color (P3): Rich and accurate color reproduction. Haptic Touch: Provides tactile feedback when interacting with the screen. Brightness: Max Brightness (Typical): 1000 nits (for everyday use). Peak Brightness (HDR): 1600 nits (for high dynamic range content). Peak Brightness (Outdoor): 2000 nits (for visibility in bright sunlight).
